What is Programming?

Essentially, is attempting to get a computer to complete a specific task without making mistakes. Computers only understand machine code, which is a numerical language known as Binary (0's and 1's).

Therefore, in order to talk to a computer you must first translate your English instructions to Binary. That is when Proramming Language comes into play. They serve as a middle-man to translate your programming into machine code. They're sort of an interpreter, converting languages (such as English) into other languages (such as machine code).
